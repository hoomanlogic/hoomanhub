—Our Vision—

At HoomanLogic, we are solely interested in designing software to be friendlier to hoomans instead of clunky interfaces that try to make hoomans think like musheens.

When musheens think more like hoomans, we can easily interact with them and get things done, instead of wasting time and effort trying to understand a musheen.

Our design principals are influenced by the latest research in neuroscience and user-interface engineering and we place the priority on understanding the needs of the user in order to be used seamlessly and effectively.

—Our Services—

Our team has a wide range of experience, especially when it comes to building business and industry-specialized solutions, with over 15 years of experience developing professional solutions and writing quality software.

We can offer custom native-OS and web-based solutions, support, and consulting.

With our strong dedication to producing software that hoomans can actually use, we take the time to carefully understand the needs of the user and conduct thorough usability tests.

We also implement best practices that make our code extremely readable, maintainable, and less prone to error.

If you are interested in an estimate, please send a detailed request to service@hoomanlogic.com.

—Our Philanthropic Quest—

We're hoomaphiles; we really love our hoomans.

That's why we spend so much of our resources building software for the business of being hooman and bridging the gap of hooman potential, always open-source, data-transparent, and ad-free, because hoomans know better than to trust what they can't see!

As fellow hoomans, we believe it is our duty to use our talents to develop systems for the benefit of all hooman-kind that create personal and social incentives to do good, provide effective and insightful monitors that enable us to learn from our past and plan a better future, and drive our progression through gradual but consistent changes to our day-to-day actions.

We depend on donations and the profits of our private-sector business to fund our philanthropic quest to help hoomans be the best hoomans they can be!
